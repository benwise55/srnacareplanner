Privacy Policy for SRNA Care Planner

Effective Date: May 22, 2026

SRNA Care Planner (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Information Collection

SRNA Care Planner does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data.

The App:

  • Does not require user accounts
  • Does not collect analytics data
  • Does not track user activity
  • Does not use advertising frameworks
  • Does not sell or share information with third parties
  • Does not transmit data to external servers

Local Data Storage

Any information you enter into the App is stored locally on your device only, if applicable, and is not accessible to the developer.

Third-Party Services

SRNA Care Planner does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children or users of any age.

Security

Because SRNA Care Planner does not collect or transmit user data, there is no user information stored on external systems.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by revising the “Effective Date” above.
